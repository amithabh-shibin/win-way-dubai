About us



Winway Auto Parts L.L.C. is a premier supplier of spare parts in the UAE, specializing in parts for Jeep, Dodge, Chrysler, and RAM (Mopar), as well as Honda and Volvo. As an authorized dealer of Trading Enterprises for Mopar and Honda parts, we pride ourselves on delivering genuine and high-quality components

Mission
To provide high-quality automobile parts and exceptional customer service, ensuring our customers’ vehicles are reliable and running smoothly

Vission
To be the leading destination for automobile parts, renowned for our extensive selection, expertise, and commitment to innovation, driving excellence in every vehicle we support

Our Expertise
We are your “Perfect Parts Consultant,” staffed by a team of professional, experienced, and technically qualified personnel dedicated to meeting all your parts requirements. Our competitive pricing and prompt delivery ensure that you receive the best service possible

Leadership
Owner and Marketing Manager, Mr. Saju Kuruvilla, is an Automobile Engineer with 30 years of experience in the industry. His background includes roles (Branch in charge, Executive Parts advisor, Field executive dealer sales) at Al-Futtaim Trading Enterprises (Distributor in UAE for Jeep, Dodge, Chrysler, Ram, Volvo & Honda), Al Rostamani (Distributor in UAE for Nissan, Renault), and Al Khoory (Distributor in UAE for Subaru), and he is a Mopar Certified Executive Parts Adviser

Inventory
Our in-stock facility includes a comprehensive range of service, engine, transmission, suspension, steering, electrical, AC & Body parts to meet diverse automotive needs
